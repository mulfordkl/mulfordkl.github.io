about me

cv | resume

I’m a Ph.D. candidate in Medical Physics at the University of Minnesota graduating in 2022. My research is mostly about building diagnostic and prognostic models of trigeminal neuralgia, but I do some cancer radiomics and ultra-high field MRI on the side. I work at the Center for Magnetic Resonance Research.

I live in St. Paul, Minnesota with my fianceé, Madelyn (a pharmacist), and our retired racing greyhound, Scout (a couch potato). My interests include cycling, history, existentialism, 20th century french organ music, and cooking.

Currently looking for a job.
